# Feedback: Please let me export my collection to CSV

## Summary

Requested by 8 users in the last month via support and Discord:

@cellar_tracker_pro: "I keep a parallel spreadsheet of my collection because I can't export from Brewboard. If you added CSV export I could ditch the spreadsheet entirely."

@homebrew_data_nerd: "I want to analyze my tasting notes in R. A simple CSV with beer name, brewery, style, rating, date added would be perfect."

## Evidence

- 8 independent requests in 30 days (support tickets #142, #156, #167, #171, #178, #183, #191, #199)
- Untappd and Vivino both offer CSV export
- Estimated effort: 1-2 days (query + streaming CSV response + download button on /collection page)

## Impact

Power users (50+ beers) are most likely to churn without this. 3 of the 8 requesters are on Pro plan. This is a retention feature, not a growth feature.
